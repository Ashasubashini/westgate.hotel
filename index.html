About Us

WELCOME TO THE WESTGATE!
For all our guests, the Westgate Beachside Hotel provides the ideal balance of pleasure and exploration. With our amazing beachfront location, you’ll wake up to beautiful coastal views and the calming sound of waves lapping on the shoreline. Your stay will be nothing less than extraordinary because of the supreme luxury and convenience offered by our elegant rooms.

Explore the amazing beauty of our seaside city, from pristine beaches ideal for water sports and sunbathing to exciting local attractions. Experience the stunning sunsets and nature's beauty.
Together, let's have some incredible experiences!
