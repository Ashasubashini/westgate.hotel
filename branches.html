Explore our incredible hotel branches around the world.

Sri lanka

Stunning beachfront hotel The West Gate in Mirissa, Sri Lanka, has breath-taking views of the Indian Ocean. It's the ideal location to unwind and take in Sri Lanka's natural beauty because it has opulent rooms, a peaceful ambiance, and easy access to the beach.

New york city

In the heart of the New York City's The West Gate offers an opulent urban getaway. It's the ideal starting point for discovering everything that NYC has to offer thanks to its chic accommodations, excellent facilities, and fantastic location.

Thailand

In the downtown area of Bangkok, Thailand, The West Gate offers an opulent and exciting experience. Elegant accommodations, first-rate food, and convenient access to historical sites make it the ideal location for soaking in Bangkok's vivacious spirit.
