Let's Explore Our Adventures

Time to have some fun!

Prepare yourselves for some thrills and excitement!
Explore the adventures and create unforgettable experiences with us. Our crew of experienced tour guides makes sure that each and every adventure is thrilling, safe, and customized to your preferences. ready to challenge yourself, enjoy the stunning surroundings, and create lifelong memories.

Water sports

With activities like snorkeling, kayaking, paddleboarding, and more, dive into adventure. Whether you're just beginning or an expert water enthusiast, our qualified staff will make sure you have an exciting and safe experience.

Boat tours

You can set off on a relaxing boat travel along the water to take in the breathtaking scenery and the cool breeze. Take in the tranquil atmosphere as you pass by iconic landmarks, snap pictures, and relax. You won't need to miss this fun trip, it's wonderful!

Beach volleyball

Play beach volleyball with your friends and show off the skills you have on the sand court. The beach volleyball court is an excellent spot to enjoy some time in the sun with loved ones, regardless of your level of experience.

Fishing trips

If you're interested in going fishing, we'll provide you with all the tools and advice you need to have a successful trip. So, gather your fishing supplies and prepare for an extraordinary day on the sea!

Beach bonfire

Beach bonfires are the coolest! cozy blankets, crackling fires, and the sound of nearby seas crashing. Bring your buddies together, toast some marshmallows, and tell some tales outside under the stars.

Spa treatments

Our opulent spa services provide a pleasant retreat from the city's bustle. Our skilled professionals will pamper you with personalised treatment, offering you everything from rejuvenating rubs to revitalising facials.

Roof top pool parties

Prepare to party on the rooftop of The West Gate beneath the stars! Our rooftop parties, which feature live music, delectable cocktails, and stunning views of the city skyline, are the most popular events in town. Don't miss up The West Gate Hotel's premier nightlife experience!

Helicopter tours

Helicopter excursions are an amazing way to see the city from a unique perspective. Take to the skies to experience breath-taking aerial views of famous landmarks, breathtaking scenery, and the bustling metropolis below.
